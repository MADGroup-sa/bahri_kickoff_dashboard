04Key Takeaways

The essentials for a successful launch.

Single Point of Contact

One person per side funneling feedback and approvals. Fast decisions keep the timeline alive.

Leadership Filming = Critical Path

6 BU leaders, 2 filming days, Riyadh. Confirming availability early unblocks the entire production pipeline.

Structured Feedback Cycles

Each approval stage has a dedicated window built into the plan. Timely feedback at each stage keeps the production pipeline moving.

What We Need From Bahri

Brand assets, ESG data (if available), B-Roll library, partner list for toolkit, leadership schedules.

Scope is Locked

Any additions post-contract are assessed and quoted separately. Out-of-scope items are clearly defined.

Internal Before External

Employees see the campaign first. Internal launch always leads external by one week.
